About Me

Short Bio

I am dedicated to advancing research in the fields of cybersecurity, trustworthy artificial intelligence, and dependable systems. My work focuses on building resilient architectures capable of withstanding emerging threats, including post-quantum paradigms and pervasive sensing risks. I lead the DependSys Lab, where we investigate theoretical and applied dimensions of secure computing.

DependSys Lab

Our lab pioneers solutions in Data-Driven Dependability and Secure Wireless Sensing. We are a collaborative environment of researchers dedicated to high-impact innovations in AI security.
